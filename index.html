Top Three Destinations in Dhaka

Visit Old Dhaka

The Lalbagh Fort is a fort in the old city of Dhaka, Bangladesh. Its name is derived from its neighbourhood Lalbagh, which means Red Garden.

Visit ational Memorial

The National Martyrs' Memorial is a national monument in Bangladesh. It was built to honour and remember those who died during the Bangladesh Liberation War in 1971.

Visit Parliament Building

Parliament of Bangladesh is a unicameral legislature consisting of 350 members of which 300 Members from 300 territorial constituencies.
